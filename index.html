Welcome to JNGEC SUNDERNAGAR

Jawaharlal Nehru Government Engineering College, Sundernagar is a government-funded engineering institute run by the government of Himachal Pradesh, in Sundernagar, Mandi district. It is affiliated to Himachal Pradesh Technical University and is approved by AICTE.
